## Full description (max 4000 chars)

```
Campus Capital is the fun way for students to get good with money — before they have any.

Most finance apps assume you already have a salary and a 401(k). Campus Capital teaches investing, budgeting, credit, startups, and career skills through the money life you actually live: campus jobs, financial aid, rent, internships, and your first paycheck. And it makes learning addictive — bite-size lessons, streaks, XP, and a winding lesson path you'll actually want to finish.

100% educational. No real money, no real trading, no risk. Every portfolio is simulated.

WHAT YOU'LL LEARN
• Money Basics — budgeting, needs vs wants, where your paycheck really goes
• Credit & Debt — credit scores, APR, student loans, paying off debt smart
• Investing — stocks, ETFs, index funds, risk, and long-term compounding
• Real Estate — the fundamentals of property and building wealth
• Startup Building — ideas, unit economics, and launching a business
• Sales & Networking — the skills that actually get you hired and funded
• Career Capital — internships, salary, and building a career that pays
• Venture Capital — how investors think and fund the next big thing

96 lessons across 8 courses, each with real examples, quizzes, and a boss challenge.

LEARN LIKE A GAME
• A Duolingo-style lesson path with locked, active, and completed stops
• Hearts, XP, daily streaks, combos, badges, and level-ups
• Five kinds of questions — multiple choice, true/false, scenarios, fill-in, and match-the-pairs
• Instant feedback that tells you the right answer and why

PRACTICE WITHOUT THE RISK
• 7 interactive simulators: build a monthly budget, choose an investing strategy, tackle credit-card debt, launch a startup, price a product, pitch an investor, and negotiate a salary
• A paper-trading simulator with real live prices and $10,000 of fake money

MORE THAN AN APP
• Daily quests and weekly goals that keep you coming back
• Campus events, clubs, and leaderboards
• Capital Coach — an AI tutor that answers money questions in plain student language
• A profile with your skill tree, badges, certificates, and saved projects

Master money before you have any. Download Campus Capital and start your first lesson free.

Educational only. Not financial advice. No real money and no real trading. All portfolios are simulated. Always do your own research before investing real money.
```
